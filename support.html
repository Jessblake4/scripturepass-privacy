ScripturePass Support

Answers to common questions. If you don't see what you need below, email us and we'll help directly.

How do I unlock a locked app?

Open ScripturePass and practice the day's verse through all three steps: Read, Recall, and Recite. Once you've recited it correctly, your locked app unlocks for a set window of time.

How do I change which apps are locked?

Go to Settings inside the app and choose which apps to shield. On iPhone this uses Apple's Screen Time permission, so you'll be asked to grant access the first time.

Why isn't blocking working on my iPhone?

Make sure you've granted Screen Time access when prompted, and that you've selected at least one app to shield in Settings. App shielding on iOS is a ScripturePass Plus feature.

How do I restore a purchase?

Open the paywall (Settings → Subscription) and tap "Restore Purchase." This restores any active subscription tied to your Apple ID.

How do I cancel my subscription?

Subscriptions are managed by Apple, not inside the app. Go to iPhone Settings → your name → Subscriptions, and cancel ScripturePass there.

How do I reset my progress?

Go to Settings → Reset All Data. This clears your streak, goal, and verse history. This cannot be undone.

What Bible translation does the app use?

ScripturePass uses the King James Version (KJV).
